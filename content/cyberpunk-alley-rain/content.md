A moody neon alley scene that holds up across aspect ratios. The key is keeping the light sources motivated — every glow comes from signage or puddle reflections.

## Base prompt

```
rain-soaked cyberpunk alley at night, holographic signage in kanji and hangul,
steam rising from street vents, lone figure with transparent umbrella,
reflections in puddles, cinematic lighting, anamorphic lens flare,
shot on Kodak Portra 800 --ar 2:3 --v 7 --style raw
```

## Variations

- Swap `lone figure with transparent umbrella` for `stray cat on a dumpster` for a quieter mood
- Add `--chaos 20` for more varied sign layouts
- For the wide establishing shot use `--ar 21:9` and drop the figure entirely

**Tip:** `--style raw` matters here — without it the neon gets over-saturated and the rain streaks turn painterly.
