ChatGPT Image 2 handles glass refraction surprisingly well if you describe the lighting rig instead of the mood.

```
Studio product photograph of an amber glass perfume bottle with a brushed
gold cap, sitting on a wet black marble slab. Single softbox key light from
the upper left, white bounce card on the right, thin rim light separating
the bottle from a charcoal background. Condensation droplets on the glass.
Sharp focus on the label, f/8 depth of field. No text on the label.
```

## Notes

- Describing the actual lighting setup (key, bounce, rim) beats adjectives like "dramatic" or "cinematic"
- Ask for `no text on the label` — otherwise it invents a garbled brand name
- Follow up with "same scene, camera 30 degrees lower, closer crop" to get a matched set
