A 10-second FPV-style shot that Kling 3 nails about 7 times out of 10. Camera language is everything — tell it what the camera does, not what the video "feels like".

```
FPV drone shot flying fast through a narrow sandstone slot canyon, camera
banking left and right following the curves of the rock, warm sunlight
shafts cutting through dust from above, motion blur on canyon walls,
smooth continuous forward motion, 10 seconds, no cuts
```

## What matters

1. `no cuts` prevents it from editing in a second angle halfway through
2. `smooth continuous forward motion` stops the drift-and-hover problem
3. Keep subjects out of it — people in fast FPV shots still warp badly
4. Generate at 10s and trim; the first second is usually a rough ramp-in
