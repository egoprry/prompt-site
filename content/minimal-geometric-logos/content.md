## Midjourney

```
flat vector logo mark, abstract geometric fox constructed from three
overlapping circles, single color on white, negative space, swiss design,
no text --ar 1:1 --v 7 --style raw --no gradients, shading, 3d
```

The `--no gradients, shading, 3d` block is what keeps it actually flat.

## ChatGPT Image 2

```
Design a minimal logo mark: an abstract fox built from exactly three
overlapping circles. Single solid black on pure white. Flat vector style,
no gradients, no outlines, no text. Center it with generous margins.
```

ChatGPT is better at "exactly three circles" — Midjourney treats counts as a suggestion. Midjourney wins on negative-space cleverness. Run both and cherry-pick.
